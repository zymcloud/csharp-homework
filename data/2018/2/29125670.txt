The shortage of livers has led most transplant centers to use extended criteria donors. Hepatitis C virus (HCV) RNA-positive donor organs are typically not given to patients who have cleared HCV. A 64-year-old male with chronic hepatitis C, genotype 1b was listed for LT with hepatocellular carcinoma. While on the waiting list, the patient was treated with sofosbuvir, ledipasvir, and ribavirin and achieved an HCV RNA
antiviral treatment; genotype 1; genotype 3; hepatocellular carcinoma; sustained virologic response.
