CD5-like (CD5L) is a soluble scavenger cysteine-rich protein that modulates inflammatory responses. We studied the involvement of CD5L in liver cancer. Immunohistochemistry (IHC) of CD5L in 60 hepatocellular carcinomas and 34 adjacent nontumor livers, showed that CD5L staining was higher in tumor than in nontumor tissue (Mann-Whitney test; P = 0.0039).
