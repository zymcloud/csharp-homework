Quercetin, a natural flavonoid, exhibits anticancer effects. The aim of this study is to determine whether the combination of quercetin with cisplatin, a conventional chemotherapeutic drug, would have synergistic suppressive effects on hepatocellular carcinoma (HCC) cells. To this end, HepG2 cells were exposed to quercetin (50 μM) or cisplatin (10 μM) alone or combination of both and cell proliferation and apoptosis were investigated.
