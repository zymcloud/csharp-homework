Primary HepatoCellular Carcinoma (PHCC) has been strongly associated with HBV and HCV infections among other aetiological factors. However; do the patients still spread the viruses? This study involved forty one Nigerian adult patients with PHCC and 45 controls who were tested for HBsAg, HBeAg, Anti-HBe, Anti-HBs, anti-HCV IgM and IgG, anti-HDV and HDV antigen using ELISA.
