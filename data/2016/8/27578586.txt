The distribution of CTCs in the peripheral blood of HCC patients were detected by CanPatrol(TM) CTCs, and its relationship with the clinical features and prognosis of the patients were analyzed.Results:s CTCs were detected in 64.5% (191/296) of the HCC patients but in none of the control group (PConclusion:Positive CTCs in the peripheral blood may indicate a poor prognosis in HCC patients.
