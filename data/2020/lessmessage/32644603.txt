Hepatocellular carcinoma (HCC) is a primary tumor of the liver and constitutes more than 90% of the primary tumor of the liver.Hepatocellular carcinoma occurs in approximately 85% of patients diagnosed with cirrhosis.HCC is now the fifth most common cause of cancer worldwide.. The second leading cause of cancer death after lung cancer in men is HCC. .Five-year survival of HCC is 18% and second to pancreatic cancer.
