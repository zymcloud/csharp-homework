Objective:To investigate the effect of Bushen Jianpi formula (BSJP) therapy on survival of the patients with moderate and advanced hepatocellular carcinoma (HCC).Methods:This was a five-year retrospective study, enrolling 209 adult patients with moderate and advanced HCC patients. Log-rank test, Cox regression analysis and Kaplan-Meier analysis was applied to analyze the data.Results:The outcome of single-factor Kaplan-Meier analysis and Log-rank test showed that stage of Barcelona-clinic liver cancer (BCLC), locoregional therapy and BSJP therapy were three factors that dominantly influenced the survival of moderate and advanced HCC patients (P Conclusion:BSJP therapy and locoregional therapy both contributed to prolonged survival for moderate and advanced HCC patients.
