Case 1: The patient was a 42-year-old man who was diagnosed with intraductal papillary-mucinous carcinoma with liver metastasis. After S-1+gemcitabine and S-1 chemotherapy, the liver metastasis had disappeared and we performed a pancreaticoduodenectomy( PD). Case 2: The patient was a 70-year-old woman who was diagnosed with pancreatic cancer with liver metastasis. After gemcitabine chemotherapy, the liver metastasis had disappeared and we performed a PD.
